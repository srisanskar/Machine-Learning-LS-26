HOSTELS
IIT Bombay has 17 Hostels to cater to the accommodation of students at IIT Bombay out of which
Hostel No 1 to 9 and 12, 13, 14, and 15 (A and B wing) are for boys whereas Hostel No 10, 10A, 11
and 15(C wing) are girls hostels.
Every hostel has washing and ironing facilities, a computer room, a TV, sports grounds and indoor
games like Table Tennis, Carrom Board, Chess etc. The access rules vary, but in general, these
facilities can be availed of against your I-Card. There’s Internet connection in each room.
Several hostels have shops in them to meet daily needs and requirements and also provide
stationary, xerox, and printing facilities.
Messes are present in each individual hostel. These messes serve food 4 times a day. There are
several additional food outlets spread around the campus if a student may choose to visit.
Further details about individual hostels can be found here

ROOM RETENTION
All students currently residing in the hostels should adhere to the following rules regarding room
retentions during the vacations:-
1. All students must submit the room retention duly signed by the respective authority by the
assigned date i.e. 25th April for summer vacation and the announced date for winter vacation.
2. If the retention is not approved, the room should be vacated by the assigned date and the keys
of the room should be handed over to the hall manager. There will be a fine of Rs 500/day in
case of noncompliance.
